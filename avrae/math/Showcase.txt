**Math** 

Some geeky utilities, for those who are into numbers. 

https://avrae.io/dashboard/workshop/671352fcaa8fe0a8d3fe37bb

* `!math` - No need to grab your calculator. Compute the trajectory of that dragon's flight path straight from avrae 
* `!convert` - Convert back and forth from various units with a simple syntax. `!convert 30ft` and figure out how much that really is. 

The math alias is a wrapper around the gvar math library. Find out how to use it to make 
other mathematically correct aliases by reading the docs: 
https://github.com/countpauper/countpauper/blob/master/avrae/math/math_lib.md
